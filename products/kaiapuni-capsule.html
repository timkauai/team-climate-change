The Kaiapuni Capsule

Our most simple box, with 5 to 6 simple eco-friendly objects, AND a carbon neutral activity, and a carbon negative activity to do that month to help our environment function its best! In buying your first box, you join our community of people trying to make a strong impact on our world!

What the box comes with

5 to 6 eco friendly items and a carbon neutral activity to do that month. For example, on your first month you would recieve:

- hat with brim made from recycled materials
- Kaiapuni Capsules Shirt
- Reef safe sunscreen
- Paracord bracelet (2), plastic necklace (1), recyclable masks (4)
- Custom calendar (photos of coral in hawaii) that specifically helps you track energy usage at home
- Jewelry made from plactic found on the beach
- 8 Kaiapuni Capsules Stickers
- Materials to plant 5 trees and how to plant them to absorb more C02

Who are we

We are a team of high school students who started this startup as part of the Nalukai Academy, a 15 day entrepreneurial camp for high school students in Hawaii. Any donation would help support and continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated. Our Team is James Pratt, Kaiulu Tiogangco, Ry Bleckel, Aaron Chang and Jade Frank.
