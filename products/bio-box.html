The Bio Box

Our most simple box, with 3 to 4 simple eco-friendly objects to help our environment function its best! In buying your first box, you join our community of people trying to make a strong impact on our world!

Read More

What the box comes with

3 to 4 eco friendly items. For example, on your first month you would receive:

- 4 stickers (two bio box stickers and two Kaiapuni Capsules stickers)
- one roll of biodegradable toilet paper
- one bar of biodegradable soap
- some soil and plants and seed for a starting garden
- 3 reusable bags

Cost: $11.99

Who are we

We are a team of high school students who started this startup as part of the Nalukai Acadamy, a 10 day entrepreneurial camp for High school students in Hawaii. Any amount would help support and continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated. Our Team is Kaiulu Tiogangco, Ry Bleckel, Aaron Chang and Jade Frank.
