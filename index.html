Meet Kaiapuni Capsules

A subscription box for those trying to make a positive impact on the Earth. We need your help to ensure Hawaii's oceans are safe for all marine life for generations to come.
Check out the Boxes

We Value our Earth

An important problem we recognized is that the rising acidity and temperature of our oceans has left our corals in extreme danger. Therefore, we created a product that will have an immense positive impact on our Earth so that our generation and the future generations can enjoy the beauty of our planet.

group

Make an Impact, Together

We want to rid people of the mindset that they alone can not solve climate change. Everyone has to do their part to reduce their carbon emissions in order to stop the planet from warming. Otherwise global warming will be imminent and inevitable. Connect using our social media and join the community of people who want to make an impact.

Helping the Earth is Fun

It's not just solving the world's problems, its having fun with it. Do fun carbon neutral and carbon negative activities with friends and family to benefit the earth and help the coral!

Year after year, humans continue to emit increasing amounts of CO2, which trap heat in the atmosphere. As a result, our ocean's temperature has risen about 1.5Â°F since 1901. This is a drastic change for marine life, as the warmer water causes coral to expel algae living inside their tissues, leaving them white and in a weakened state, a phenomenon known as coral bleaching.
